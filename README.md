**Meeting Title**: AI Inference Distribution Network

**Meeting Description**: 

Artificial Intelligence is often divided into two essential phases: AI Training and AI Inference. While training focuses on building models, inference is where the real-world value emerges—by applying those models to generate insights, support decisions, and enable intelligent applications. This side meeting, titled "AI Inference Distribution Network," is dedicated entirely to advancing the field of AI inference. We will explore a range of key topics, including emerging inference scenarios, innovative AI inference network architectures, model/inference distribution mechanisms, scheduling mechanisms that enable efficient and scalable deployment. We are open to speaker proposals that align with these topics.
